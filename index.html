# Rankings & Tiebreakers

Players are ranked by total points, summed across all six of their drafted countries.

When two or more players are tied on points, goal difference (total goals scored minus goals conceded across all of a player's countries) breaks the tie — higher wins.

If players are still level on both points and goal difference, they share the same rank.

Points and goal difference both update live during in-progress matches, so rankings can shift in real time.
